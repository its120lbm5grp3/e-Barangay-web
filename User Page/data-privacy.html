Data Privacy Act of 2012

The Barangay Poblacion 3, Tiaong, Quezon upholds the principles of the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), which aims to protect the fundamental human right of privacy while ensuring the free flow of information for innovation and growth. We are committed to safeguarding the personal information of our constituents and stakeholders.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect only the necessary personal data required to process official transactions such as resident enlistment, document requests, and barangay services. All data collected will be treated with strict confidentiality and used solely for legitimate purposes.

2. Use and Processing of Information

Personal information provided will be processed in accordance with the law. It will only be used for official barangay records, service delivery, and other authorized legal purposes.

3. Data Protection Measures

The barangay implements physical, technical, and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Personal data will not be shared with any third party without your consent, except when required by law, court orders, or government regulations.

5. Rights of Data Subjects

You have the right to access, correct, and withdraw your personal information, as well as the right to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission if you believe your data privacy rights have been violated.

6. Retention and Disposal

Your personal information will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes of collection or as required by law. Secure disposal procedures will be followed once data is no longer needed.

7. Contact Information

For any questions, requests, or concerns regarding your personal data, you may contact us at barangaypoblaciontres.tiaong@gmail.com or visit the Barangay Hall at J.P. Rizal Street, Tiaong, Quezon.
